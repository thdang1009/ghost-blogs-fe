Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data from Ghost Site

At Ghost Site, we respect your privacy and give you control over your personal data. If you have connected your Facebook account or used Facebook Login with our service, you have the right to request deletion of your data.

Option 1: Delete your account through our website

You can delete your entire account and all associated data by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your Ghost Site account
  2. Navigate to "My Account" or "Profile Settings"
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  4. Click on "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision by following the prompts

Once deleted, all your personal information will be removed from our systems.

Option 2: Contact us directly

If you prefer, you can request data deletion by contacting us directly:

  1. Send an email to thdang1009@gmail.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days

Data Retention

Please note that some information may be retained in backup copies for a limited period but will not be available online. Additionally, we may retain certain information to prevent fraud, assist with investigations, enforce our terms of use, or comply with legal requirements.

Facebook Data

If you used Facebook Login with our service and want to revoke access, you can also manage app permissions through your Facebook account:

  1. Visit Facebook Applications Settings
  2. Find Ghost Site in your list of connected apps
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access

Please note that removing our app from Facebook will not delete your account on our platform. To completely delete your data, please follow Option 1 or Option 2 above.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or privacy practices, please contact us at thdang1009@gmail.com.
